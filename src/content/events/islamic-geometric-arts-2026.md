Join us for a creative and relaxing workshop where you'll discover the beauty of Islamic geometric art.

We'll begin with a short talk about the history and ideas behind these timeless patterns. Then, you'll try drawing and coloring your own simple four-fold geometric design to take home!

## For Families — Everyone Welcome!

Our workshop offers a variety of activities for different age groups — kids, teens, and adults. Even children under 5 can join in!

No experience needed! This hands-on session is designed for absolute beginners.

## Event Details

- **Date:** Saturday, 27 June 2026
- **Time:** 16:30 – 19:30 (feel free to leave earlier if you need to)
- **Venue:** Kanazawa Umar bin Al-Khattab Mosque
- **Languages:** Japanese, English

## Tools to Bring

Art supplies will be provided on site on demand, but feel free to bring your own if you'd like:

- Ruler (30 cm length at least)
- Compass
- Pencil
- Eraser
- Tracing paper
- Carbon paper
- Drawing paper
- Coloring supplies (any kind you prefer)
