Assalamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatuh,

Alhamdulillah, we are pleased to announce that **Eid ul-Adha 1447H has been confirmed on Wednesday, 27th May 2026** in Japan. Eid prayer will be held at **Kanazawa Umar bin Al-Khattab Mosque**.

This confirmation is based on the announcement by **Ruyat Hilal Committee Japan (R.H.C.J.)**: the sighting of the Hilal (Crescent) was confirmed by the Islamic Authorities in Malaysia on 29 Dhul Qa'dah 1447 A.H. (17 May 2026). In accordance with the Fatwa of the International Islamic Fiqh Academy and the scholars, we follow the Malaysia Islamic authorities in this case. Accordingly, **1st Dhul Hijjah 1447H falls on Monday, 18th May 2026**, and Eid ul-Adha on **Wednesday, 27th May 2026**.

## Prayer Sessions

To accommodate all community members comfortably, Eid prayer will be held in **three sessions**:

| Session | Time | Imam |
|---------|------|------|
| Session 1 | 6:30 AM | Imam Ilyas Maulana |
| Session 2 | 7:30 AM | Ahmad |
| Session 3 | 8:30 AM | Ahmad Sohib |

Please arrive **15 minutes before** your chosen session. Doors will open 30 minutes prior to each session.

## What to Bring

- Prayer mat (sajadah) — recommended but not required
- Clean clothes (preferably best attire)

## Registration

To help us plan the space and prepare for each session, **we kindly ask all attendees to register in advance**.

Registration takes less than a minute. Please indicate the number of people in your group and your preferred session.

## Additional Notes

- Since a large number of worshippers are expected, please **perform wudu (ablution) before leaving home**. After the prayer, kindly leave the mosque promptly to make room for the next session and avoid congestion that may disturb the surrounding neighbourhood.
- **Buses may be full** on the day. If you are travelling in a group, consider sharing a taxi as an alternative.
- Please review our [mosque general rules](/#mosque-rules) before attending.

## Location

Kanazawa Umar bin Al-Khattab Mosque, Kanazawa, Ishikawa, Japan.
