Assalamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatuh,

Alhamdulillah, as the blessed month of Ramadan draws to a close, we are pleased to announce the Eid Al-Fitr 1447H prayer arrangements at **Kanazawa Umar bin Al-Khattab Mosque**.

> **Note:** The exact date of Eid Al-Fitr is pending the result of rukyatul hilal (moon sighting). Please follow the announcement from the [Ruyat Hilal Committee Japan](/news/1447-rukyatul-hilal).

## Prayer Sessions

To accommodate all community members comfortably, Eid prayer will be held in **three sessions**:

| Session | Time | Imam |
|---------|------|------|
| Session 1 | 6:30 AM | Imam Ilyas Maulana |
| Session 2 | 7:30 AM | Ahmad |
| Session 3 | 8:30 AM | Ammar Abdurrahman Hasyim|

Please arrive **15 minutes before** your chosen session. Doors will open 30 minutes prior to each session.

## What to Bring

- Prayer mat (sajadah) — recommended but not required
- Clean clothes (preferably new or best attire)
- Sadaqah / Zakat Al-Fitr (if not yet distributed)

## Registration

To help us plan the space and prepare for each session, **we kindly ask all attendees to register in advance**.

[Register here](/register/eid-fitr-1447)

Registration takes less than a minute. Please indicate the number of people in your group and your preferred session.

## Location

Kanazawa Umar bin Al-Khattab Mosque, Kanazawa, Ishikawa, Japan.

May Allah accept our fasts, prayers, and good deeds throughout Ramadan. We look forward to celebrating Eid with the community.
